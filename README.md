# wheatdb
This is a variant database of wheat. It has variant (SNPs and InDels) informations of 27 _T. Sphaerococcum_ (Indian dwarf wheat); 126 traditional wheat landraces grown across major wheat-producing states of India; 27 varieties released prior to the green revolution (IPR) in India; and 42 varieties developed during and post green revolution. It represents diverse cultivation periods and agro-climatic zones across India.

The database is originally developed in collaboration with [Rammani Biswas](https://github.com/Rammani10Biswas) and maintained by me.
The data are generated and maintained in the lab of Dr. Debasis Chattopadhyay, Director (Additional Charge), National Institute of Plant Genome Research, New Delhi, India.
