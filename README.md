# wheatdb
This is a variant database of wheat. It has variant (SNPs and InDels) informations of 126 traditional wheat landraces grown across major wheat-producing states of India; 27 varieties released prior to the green revolution (IPR) in India; and 42 varieties developed during and post green revolution. It represents diverse cultivation periods and agro-climatic zones across India.

It is originally developed in collaboration with [Rammani Biswas](https://github.com/Rammani10Biswas) during her M.Sc. rogramme.
